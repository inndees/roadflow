Build an AI-Powered Civil Construction Scheduling Platform
Create a web application called RoadFlow AI.
Problem
Civil engineering and road construction projects often experience delays because project managers must manually update schedules when unexpected events occur.
Examples:
* Rain delays
* Material shortages
* Worker absences
* Equipment breakdowns
* Late deliveries
* Site access restrictions
Current scheduling software is often complicated, expensive, and requires experienced planners to manually adjust timelines.
The goal of roadflow AI is to automatically generate and update project schedules using AI.

When selecting (+project), you will be able to choose between 3 types of projects:
- type 1: coat the road and then laying new road on top (can input what type of material will be used)
- type 2: remove the old road then recycling it with new road to product more material with similar quality as new road without wasting material, then layering it out onto the area removed. 
- type 3: deep into system (renovating pipes and more) and removing a cross section of deep of road.

Core Concept
A project manager enters:
* Road length
* Road type
* Cross Sectional Area/ type of shap
* Number of workers
* machinery
* materials
* Working hours
* per hour, how much km can be done (give maximum and minimum)
* Planned start date

The AI automatically creates a construction timeline.
When conditions change, the AI instantly recalculates the schedule and provides a revised timeline.
